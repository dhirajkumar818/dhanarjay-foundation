WHO WE ARE

A Mission Rooted in Compassion & Integrity

Dhanarjay Welfare Foundation is a Section 8 Company dedicated to delivering compassionate rehabilitation and healthcare services to underserved populations in rural Bihar.

Through our rehabilitation center, we provide critical physical and emotional recovery support. Our focus extends to women empowerment, sustainable agriculture, and community education to ensure holistic development.
