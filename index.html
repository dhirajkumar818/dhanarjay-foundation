About Us

Dhanarjay Welfare Foundation is a Section 8 NGO committed to delivering compassionate rehabilitation and healthcare services to underserved populations in rural Bihar. We provide critical physical and emotional recovery support through our rehabilitation center, helping individuals regain independence and dignity.

CIN: U88900BR2025NPL074863 | DARPAN ID: BR/2025/0591108

Our Focus Areas

1. Rehabilitation & Healthcare

Free medical camps, physiotherapy, occupational therapy for elderly & disabled. Basic healthcare in remote villages.

2. Women Empowerment

Skill training in tailoring, digital literacy, entrepreneurship for rural women. 25 women trained in tailoring/agriculture.

3. Sustainable Agriculture

Supporting small farmers with seeds, tools, training. 15 farmers provided with resources to increase yield.

4. Community Education

Workshops on hygiene, legal rights, child safety, nutrition, and digital inclusion for responsible citizens.
