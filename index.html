About Dhanarjay Welfare Foundation

We are a dedicated Section 8 non-governmental organization committed to delivering compassionate rehabilitation and healthcare services to underserved populations in rural Bihar.

Our work is rooted in integrity, care, and deep community service. Through our rehabilitation center, we provide critical physical and emotional recovery support to help individuals regain independence and dignity.

Our Key Focus Areas

Healthcare & Rehabilitation

Free medical camps, physiotherapy, occupational therapy for elderly & disabled in remote villages.

Women Empowerment

Skill training in tailoring, digital literacy & entrepreneurship to make rural women self-reliant.

Sustainable Agriculture

Supporting small farmers with training, seeds, tools & market access to increase yield & reduce debt.

Community Education

Awareness workshops on hygiene, legal rights, child safety, nutrition & digital inclusion.
